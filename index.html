About Me

Hello! I am Princess Kosim, born on October 11th, 2006. Currently residing in the vibrant city of Medan, I am pursuing my Bachelor's degree at Universitas Pelita Harapan Medan. I am part of the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, majoring in Information Systems.

My passion for technology and innovation has driven me to explore the world of AI, and I am eager to bring that enthusiasm to the Google Student Ambassador program. I believe that with the right tools and community, we can empower students in Medan to become leaders in the digital age.

I am excited to be part of the GSA 2026 journey, where I hope to grow, learn, and contribute to our shared goals. Let's make this an unforgettable experience together!

Beyond academics, I am a tech enthusiast who loves to stay updated with the latest trends in AI and software development. I enjoy experimenting with new technologies and sharing my findings with my peers. My goal is to create a vibrant tech community at UPH Medan where students can collaborate, innovate, and thrive.

When I'm not coding or studying, you can find me curating my Spotify playlists or sharing snippets of my life on Instagram. Music and social connections are a big part of who I am, and I love sharing that with others.
